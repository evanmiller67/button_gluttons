Welcome to ButtonGluttons

What is ButtonGluttons?

ButtonGluttons is a demo Code PaLOUsa app to increase your chances of winning a grand prize drawing given away by CafePress.com. You're automatically entered to win just by registering. Your buttons are actually raffle tickets for the drawing.

Getting started

Here’s how to get rolling:

  1. Register

    All we want is your name and email address. We wont spam you, but will send your game stats.

  2. Gameplay

    • You may roll for a weapon 3 times
    • Only your most current roll counts
    • Whoever has the better weapon, wins
    • Take one of their buttons to shame them and show your button gluttony
    • Play until you have no more badges
    • Profit!

  3. Bosses - CafePress Employees

    • Count for 2 buttons
    • Have scarce buttons
